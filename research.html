What We Investigate

Research

Prof. Wonyong Choi's group investigates semiconductor-based photo(electro)chemical systems addressing environmental and energy challenges.

The solar-driven photo(electro)catalytic chemical process is a key technology for sustainable utilization of solar energy. It has been widely applied to environmental remediation and solar fuel production. Our research spans five interconnected areas, all centered on harnessing light energy for chemistry.
